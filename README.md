# SaaS Revenue Risk Detection & Churn Reduction Strategy

##  Problem Statement

SaaS businesses often face revenue loss due to customer churn and over-dependence on a small number of high-value accounts.
This project analyzes subscription, user, and product usage data to identify revenue risks and churn drivers.

---

##  Objective

* Identify revenue concentration risk across accounts
* Detect high-value accounts at risk of churn
* Analyze relationship between product engagement and revenue
* Provide insights to support retention strategy

---

##  Dataset Overview

* 700+ Users
* 180 Accounts
* 200+ Subscriptions
* 1200+ Product Events

---

##  Key Insights

* Top 20% of accounts contribute **83% of total revenue**, indicating high dependency risk
* ₹82K revenue (~16% of total MRR) identified as **at risk from low-engagement accounts**
* Weak correlation (**0.20**) between engagement and revenue
* Enterprise segment contributes ~74% of total revenue

---

##  Business Impact

* High dependency on a small number of accounts increases financial risk
* Potential revenue loss if high-value accounts churn
* Need to prioritize retention for enterprise customers
* Opportunity to improve monetization in mid-market segment

---
